Let's Celebrate Garfield the Cat!

Some things to know about this "holiday":

  • National Garfield the Cat Day is celebrated on June 19th.
  • The holiday is used to acknowledge the creation of the Garfield comic strip by Jim Davis and the entertainment it brings us.
  • Did you know? Garfield is a tabby cat! He also hates Mondays. He has also spawned an unimaginable amount of memes such as the popular "Garfield Minus Garfield" parody strip.
