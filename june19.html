Welcome to the Juneteenth Celebration!

Some things to know about this holiday:

  • Juneteenth is celebrated as the anniversary of the order by Major General Gordon proclaiming freedom for enslaved poeple in Texas on June 19th, 1865.
  • It's name is derived from the combination of June and nineteenth
  • Originating in Galveston, Juneteenth is also commonly used as a day to celebrate African-American culture.
  • Juneteenth was signed into law and recognized as a federal holiday in 2021, however it has been celebrated by many since 1865.
  • Juneteenth is also known to some as Freedom Day.
